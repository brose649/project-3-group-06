UFO Sightings Map

Welcome to the UFO Sightings Map! This interactive map allows you to explore reported UFO sightings across the United States. To begin, use the filters on the left to narrow down the sightings by selecting a specific state and/or the shape of the object observed. Once filtered, the map will update to show markers indicating where these sightings were reported. You can click on any marker to see more detailed information about the sighting, including the date, shape, and any additional comments provided by the observer. Additionally, you can toggle the heatmap layer to visualize the density of sightings across different regions, giving you a quick overview of hotspots where UFO activity has been most frequently reported. This tool is designed to help you investigate patterns and trends in UFO sightings with ease. Have fun exploring, and who knows, you might uncover something extraordinary!
